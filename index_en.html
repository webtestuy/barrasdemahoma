Establecimiento
Barras de Mahoma

Establecimiento Barras de Mahoma is a family field dedicated to livestock, agriculture, viticulture and forestry. It is located in Uruguay, at the Sierras de Mahoma, in the department of San José and 120 kilometers from the capital of the country, Montevideo.

The Sierras de Mahoma constitute a wide rock formation that, at the same time having a great visual charm, offers an excellent soil for the cultivation of the vine.

About Us

The Vineyard

The history of this vineyard goes back to 2001, when the family, due to the great potential they discovered in this geographical area, decided to make the first vineyard plantation, with three red strains of French origin, Cabernet Sauvignon, Cabernet Franc and Tannat.
